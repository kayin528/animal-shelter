Welcome to Didier's Animal Shelter

We have a wide selection of cuddly, adorable puppies available for adoption.

  • Tickles, the formidable boxer

    • Meet Tickles. With paws that look like they’ve been dipped in white chocolate and eyes the color of Hershey’s kisses, Allie looks like a candy bar covered in fur! This sweeter-than-sugar gal is a 7-year old, 45-pound Boxer who is pretty, perky, and packed with personality!

  • Pebbles, the ravishing Bulldog

    • Nutty...but in a good way! That’s Walnut, for ya! This 10-month old, 35-pound, tan-coated, bulldog is silly, playful, energetic, charming and handsome...and is seeking a love that’s pure and true.

    An ideal day for Walnut would include a visit to the dog park, lots of treats, plenty of cuddles, a nice long stroll through the ‘hood, a chicken-n-kibble dinner with a side of fresh water ;-), and a belly rub right before bedtime. It doesn’t take much to make this bubbly boy beam!

  • Lucky, the hunting labrador

    • Let me introduce you to Gracie, and what a stunning pooch she is! Boasting a dark ebony coat, with beautiful be-speckled paws, and the deepest brown eyes you’ve ever seen, this girl is simply gorgeous!

    During her time at Bideawee, Gracie has already won over the staff and volunteers with her sweet personality!This sweet girl is definitely shy at first, but once she knows you, her sparkling personality shines bright!

    Gracie is an active girl, who would love a family that would enjoy spending time taking her on long walks, and exploring different places?
